Elias Stemeseder (*1990) is a pianist, keyboard instrumentalist, electronic musician and composer who seeks to combine musical processes, disciplines and tools from diverse temporal and geographic origins to create personal sets of constraints and order. Stemeseder is a recording artist for INTAKT records.
