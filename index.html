Elias Stemeseder (*1990) is a pianist, keyboard instrumentalist, electronic musician

and composer with an interest in combining musical processes, disciplines and tools

from diverse temporal and geographic origins to create novel sets of constraints and

order for improvised performances. Stemeseder is a recording artist for INTAKT records
